Our Mission

At EcoCollect, our mission is to create a circular economy by providing a seamless platform for individuals and businesses to buy, sell, and recycle e-waste. We believe in reducing waste and giving electronics a second life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where electronic waste is minimized, and every device is utilized to its full potential. By connecting sellers with buyers and recyclers, we aim to build a sustainable community that values environmental responsibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions.

What kind of items can be listed?

We exclusively focus on e-waste. This includes used electronics like phones, laptops, components, and other devices.

Can I list items in bulk?

Yes, our platform supports listings for bulk quantities of e-waste materials.

Do you provide pickup and drop-off services?

No, EcoCollect does not provide any logistics services. Our platform's main purpose is to connect you directly with recyclers and buyers. You must arrange pickup or delivery with them.

Are the rates negotiable?

Prices are set by the sellers and are subject to market fluctuations. Once a price is agreed upon between a buyer and seller, it is fixed for that transaction. Different rates may apply for larger, bulk quantities.
