Who We Are

Our Mission

At EcoCollect, our mission is to create a circular economy by providing a seamless platform for individuals and businesses to buy, sell, and recycle pre-owned goods. We believe in reducing waste, conserving resources, and giving products a second life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where waste is minimized, and every item is utilized to its full potential. By connecting sellers with buyers and recyclers, we aim to build a sustainable community that values conscious consumption and environmental responsibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions.

What kind of scrap do you take?

We primarily facilitate the exchange of e-waste and other electronic items.

Do you take scrap in bulk quantity?

Yes, our platform supports listings for bulk quantities of scrap materials.

Do you charge for doorstep pickup?

No, we do not currently provide pickup services. Our platform's main purpose is to connect you directly with recyclers and buyers, who may offer their own pickup services.

Are your rates negotiable?

The prices are subject to change based on market fluctuations in the scrap industry. Once a price is agreed upon between a buyer and seller, it will remain unchanged for that transaction. Different rates may apply for larger, bulk quantities.
